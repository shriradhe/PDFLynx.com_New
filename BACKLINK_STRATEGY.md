Below is your step-by-step outreach strategy and the templates you can use to start building your domain authority.

Many tech blogs and productivity websites maintain listicles like *"Top 10 Free Tools for Students"* or *"Best Alternatives to Adobe Acrobat"*. You should reach out to the authors of these articles and pitch PDFLynx as a free, privacy-focused alternative.

## Strategy 2: Broken Link Building (Highly Effective)
Find blogs in the productivity/tech space that are linking to old PDF tools that no longer exist, have shut down, or are now completely paywalled. Let the author know their link is dead and offer PDFLynx as the replacement.

### Template B: The Broken Link Pitch
**Subject:** Heads up: Broken link on your [Topic] post
**Body:**
> Hi [Author Name],
> 
> I absolutely loved your guide on [Article Topic] — especially the section about [Specific Detail].
